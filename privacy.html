Privacy Policy

At Tanzai Tools, part of Tanzai Labs, we take your privacy seriously. Our philosophy is simple: Your data belongs to you.

1. Serverless Processing

Unlike other platforms, 100% of our tools operate client-side. This means any data you input (images, text, JSON, or passwords) is processed directly in your browser. Nothing is ever uploaded to our servers.

2. No Data Collection

We do not collect, store, or share any personal information. We don't use databases to save your inputs, ensuring total anonymity while you use our premium utilities.

3. Cookies & Ads

We may use cookies only for basic site analytics and to serve relevant advertisements via partners like Google AdSense to keep this platform free for everyone.
